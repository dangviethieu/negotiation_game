Welcome to this study. The study examines how individuals make negotiation decisions in a moral dilemma.
You now take part in an online negotiation game. You will be assigned randomly a role either “buyer” or “seller” by computer. The decisions you make will be anonymised and no data at any stage can be used to identify you.
Please read and sign consent form below before starting the study.
